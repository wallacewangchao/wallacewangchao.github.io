I am a Senior Research Scientist (HCI/XAI) at Honda Research. My recent work focuses on human-AI interaction applied on autonomous driving vehicle and robot (see my Google Scholar) .

I got my PhD from Eindhoven University of Technology (the Netherlands) in the direction of HCI for intelligent vehicle. My PhD research received multiple paper awards at Automotive UI and were featured by Engadget/new Scientist. Some of my design implementation were exhibited in Frankfurt Auto Show and Dutch Design Week.

Before my PhD I worked I as a UX designer at Shanghai Motor Group. Before joining Honda, I worked as Creative Technologist at NIO. Insistence on HCI of automotive domain for 10 years gives me profound insight into the trends of intelligent system.

I have a combined background of design, human-computer interaction and computer science. (see my LinkedIn) As a designer at heart, I believe AI would be a new engine for the next-generation human-machine interface.
